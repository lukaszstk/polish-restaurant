Pierogi with Potatos and Cheese

Dough
3 cups (367g) Any All-Purpose Flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 large egg
1/2 cup (112g) sour cream
4 tablespoons (58g) butter, room temperature

Filling
1 cup (226g) mashed potatoes, warm
1 cup (113g) sharp cheddar cheese, shredded

To finish
4 tablespoons (57g) butter, cold
2 large (156g) shallots, diced; or one medium (156g) onion, sliced
